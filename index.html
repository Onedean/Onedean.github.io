About Me


I am Wei Chen (陈伟), a first year Ph.D. Student at CityMind Lab of System Hub affiliated with Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou), supervised by Prof. Yuxuan Liang. I am currently working on the research, development, and innovation of spatio-temporal data mining and AI, with a broad range of applications in smart cities. Prior to that, I was obtained my Master degree at School of Information Science and Engineering, Ocean University of China, supervised by Prof. Yanwei Yu. I also worked closely with Principal Researcher Justin Ding at Microsoft Research Asia. I have published some papers in refereed journals (e.g., FGCS) and conferences (e.g. IJCAI). I also served as a sub-reviewer for some prestigious conferences or journals, including KDD, WWW, AAAI, WSDM, and ACM TIST.

My research interests include (but are not limited to) machine learning, causal discovery, spatiotemporal data modeling, and intelligent transportation systems. I am a strong advocate of open-source and reproducible research. To contribute more ideas to the research community, I am trying to make the datasets and codes of our research publicly available on GitHub.
